## 1. Prohibited Content

You may not create, upload, generate, host, distribute, or link to content that:

### 1.1 Illegal Content

- Violates any applicable law or regulation
- Constitutes child sexual abuse material (CSAM) — **zero tolerance**, reported to NCMEC and law enforcement
- Promotes, facilitates, or instructs the commission of crimes, terrorism, or violence
- Infringes intellectual property rights (copyright, trademark, patent, trade secret)
- Violates the privacy or publicity rights of any person
- Constitutes defamation, libel, or slander
- Constitutes fraud, identity theft, or impersonation

### 1.2 Harmful Content

- Promotes self-harm, suicide, or eating disorders
- Promotes or glorifies violence against persons or animals
- Constitutes harassment, bullying, or threats
- Constitutes hate speech or incites discrimination based on race, ethnicity, national origin, religion, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, or other protected characteristic
- Sexually exploits or sexualizes minors in any way
- Includes non-consensual intimate imagery ("revenge porn")
- Promotes dangerous activities, dangerous goods, or weapons of mass destruction

### 1.3 Adult and Sensitive Content

- Pornographic, sexually explicit, or sexually suggestive content
- Gratuitous violence or gore
- Content depicting cruelty to animals

### 1.4 Deceptive Content

- Misinformation, disinformation, or content designed to mislead
- Deepfakes or synthetic media designed to deceive
- False advertising or misleading product claims
- Phishing attempts or scams
- Fake reviews, ratings, or social proof
- Impersonation of any person, brand, or government entity

### 1.5 Regulated Content

Content that requires regulatory authorization that you do not have, including:

- Unlicensed financial advice or trading platforms
- Unlicensed medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment
- Unlicensed legal advice
- Unlicensed gambling or lottery
- Sale of regulated goods (firearms, controlled substances, prescription drugs, alcohol to minors, tobacco to minors)

### 3.1 Sexual Content Involving Minors

We have a **zero-tolerance** policy for child sexual abuse material ("CSAM"). Any user who creates, uploads, distributes, or attempts to generate CSAM will be:

- Immediately and permanently banned
- Reported to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC)
- Reported to law enforcement
- Subject to civil and criminal liability

We use automated tools and human review to detect CSAM. If you suspect CSAM on Appio, report it immediately to {{ABUSE_EMAIL}} or via NCMEC's CyberTipline.

### 3.2 Critical Infrastructure and High-Risk Use Cases

You may not use Appio to develop or deploy applications that:

- Control critical infrastructure (power, water, transportation, healthcare delivery)
- Make medical diagnoses or treatment decisions
- Make legal determinations
- Make hiring, firing, or significant employment decisions
- Make credit, insurance, or other consequential financial decisions
- Are used in safety-critical contexts where errors could cause physical harm

Generated Apps are not safety-critical software. They are not certified or validated for any regulated use.

### 3.3 Surveillance

You may not use Appio to build apps that:

- Conduct mass surveillance
- Track individuals without their consent
- Profile individuals based on protected characteristics
- Facilitate stalking or harassment

### 3.4 Election Integrity

You may not use Appio to:

- Create content designed to suppress voter turnout
- Spread false information about elections, voting, or candidates
- Impersonate political figures, parties, or election officials

We are not obligated to provide notice or an opportunity to cure before taking action.
